Expansion Of Church Premises

The assemblies of God Rumuagholu 1 church has successfully acquired a neighbouring piece of land in a bid to expand the church premises and make room for other forms of church structures. The piece of land which has been a long-term target of the church, was finally acquired under the supervision of the senior pastor of the church Reverend Anthony Asuquo.

Reverend Anthony Asuquo upon his arrival, made clear to the church and its members of his desire to purchase the piece of land. In no time, plans were put in place to internally generate funds in order to achieve this goal. With total support from the church and its members the required amount of money needed was generated and The Assemblies of God Rumuagholu 1 church became the rightful owners of the piece of land.

The Rumuagholu 1 Church with a burning desire for expansion, swiftly worked on the piece of land and it currently serves as a parking space for the church. Also in an attempt to further utilise the land, the church embarked on an ongoing project to build its state-of-the-art restrooms. The project which started at the beginning of the year is almost completed and in no time would become functional.

with several other plans and projects ahead, the church is constantly making progress in achieving is desired goal of expansion.
