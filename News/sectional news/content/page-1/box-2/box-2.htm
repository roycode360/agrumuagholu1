Our Big Daddy Celeberates his 59th Birthday!

On sunday the 29th of December 2019, our father, the sectional leader and senior pastor of the Rumuagholu 1 section Reverend Anthony Asuquo, along with the entire church congregation,celebrated his 59th birthday.

Although the actual birth date fell on saturday the 28th of December, he decided to shift celebrations to the following day in order to involve the church.

It was a wonderful occassion and a day of laughter, joy and thanksgiving.

The birthday celebration involved Various activities including cutting of the bithday cake, a general toast in honor of the celebrant, as well as several sets of group photographs with family and friends.

There was also a special session of thanksgiving where our big daddy Reverend Anthony Asuquo (the celebrant), along side his wife Mrs Evelyn Asuquo and family, rendered their special thanks to God for yet another successful year and birthday.

The thanksgiving session was accompanied by prayers and proclaimation of blessings from the church congregation upon the celebrant and his family. This was led by one of the elders in the church.

To round up the birthday ceremony was an intense session of worship and praise. The entirechurch sand and danced to the glory of God.

Reverend Anthony Asuquo, who has only been in charge of the Rumuagholu 1 church for a few years, has in his short time transformed the church positively to a whole new level. He is a father, a leader and a mentor.

We pray for more grace, strength and favour upon his life to continue the good work! happy birthday once again!
