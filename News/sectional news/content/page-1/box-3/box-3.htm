Abasio: Latest song by gospel artist Fjoez

Port Harcourt's uprising gospel artist Friday Joseph, popularly known as Fjoez, few hours ago released yet another thrilling song titled "Abasio".

Fjoez who is already a popular gospel figure in Port Harcourt has released several amazing songs including Obimo, Solid Rock and Ebenezer, where he featured the H.R.P.C choir.

Currently the district choir director of Ikwerre west choir and the H.R.P.C of Assemblies Of God, Fjoez has experienced a tangible growth in his gospel music ministry and also in popularity over the last few years. This is evident as he attracted a record deal with Dafdoxa records late last year, and has been working with them till date, with songs like Ebenezer and Abasio produced by Dafdoxa records.

The powerful new single Abasio is a soul lifting song. A complete reminder that God is our only reliable hope and anchor. It is the perfect song to strengthen our faith in this period of fear and panic.
