Assemblies Of God church leader prophesies as men’s convention ends

The National Men’s Convention of the Assemblies of God Church which was conducted at Benin City, Edo State, and had an attendance of over 9,000 participants coming from 126 different units in the 36 states of Nigeria, ended in a glorious way.

General superintendent of the church, Dr. Chidi Okoroafor, has prayed God to authenticate the participants’ evidence. He gave a prophecy that is expected to come true before the end of October: “I see a great awakening among the men; I saw them taking up their rightful leads. The evidence we are gathered to look at is gospel evidence. When you are talking about evidence, you are talking about proof.”

Worshippers at the event were afforded the opportunity to reconnect with their Maker and to pray for the country. The convention tagged ‘Gospel Evidence’ started on Monday, July 22 at Assemblies of God Evangel Camp Ground, Benin City.
