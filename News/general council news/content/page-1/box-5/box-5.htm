Peniel 2019 tagged "Injury Time"

The Assemblies Of God Church conduced their annual PENIEL program for the year 2019, tagged "INJURY TIME " . The venue as usual was the National Convention Center of Assemblies of God Nigeria EVANGEL CAMP Okpoto, in Ebonyi state and it started from the 18th, november (Monday) to the 22nd november (Friday). The program attracted several resounding guest speakers and annoited ministers of the gospel from different parst of the world. The cream of ministers, amongst several others were:
Rev Dr Charles Osueke (Grandfather of AG)
Rev Dr Uma Ukpai
Rev Dr John Aniemeke
Rev Dr Clay (General Superintendent Assembles of God USA)
Rev Dr Gregg Beggs
Rev Dr Godwin Amaowoh
Rev Dr Vincent Alaje
Rev Pst Ejikeme Ejim
Rev. Chidi Okoroafor PhD (General Superintendent Assemblies of God Nigeria).

The 2019 PENIEL program conisisted of various sections of prayers, sermons, teachings and ministrations in music, drama and other forms.

The General Superintendent of Assemblies of God USA Dr Douglas Clay with his team of ministers arrived at the National Convention Center of Assemblies of God Nigeria EVANGEL CAMP Okpoto, in Ebonyi state and was welcomed by the General Council officers led by the General Superintendent Rev Chidi Okoroafor at the EVANGEL CAMP Okpoto. Several other ministers and invited guests arrived just in time for the 2019 PENIEL program . During the program, the General Superintendent Rev. Dr Chidi Okoroafor officially announced the 2020 Theme of the church as "EXTRA MILE FOR THE SAKE OF THE GOSPEL". In the same spirit Minister Temple Cee a music minister based in the city of Portharcourt presented the official song in respect to the theme of next year according to the leading of the Holy Ghost.

In one of the events of the program, missionaries from Kebbi state were presented and with them, all the who gave thier lives to christ as a result of hearing the word of God preached by the missionaries. It was a glorious moment as the entire congregation expressed thier gratitude and thanksgiving to God for the new souls that have been won into the kingdom of God. Many of them gave Testimonies of how God saved them and promised God never to go back to sin.

One of the testimonies was from a young Teenager who said 'When you have bad friends around you, You are bound to have the worst future ever but thank God for making me to be in a Programme organized by the Teens Challenge. The Nigerian Teens Cordinator Challenge Mr David Omih promised the General Council that very soon the Teens challenge will get to all the district of the general council but for the main time it is still at the national level.

The General Superintendent of Assemblies of God Nigeria Rev. Dr Chidi Okoroafor who doubles as the Chairman of Central Assemblies of God Alliance Africa during a sermon section, spoke on the topic "ELEVEN FORTY-FIVE", and generated his bible text from Text Gen 16:1-16; 18:9. In his message, he stressed the importance of christians being faithful and patient with God until the end, sayin "The story of your life does not conclude where God wants you to be; Any day you put FULL STOP where God has put comma it then means you will die Young. Do not try to assist God in the affairs of your life because he knows the end from the beginning".

Other ministers and guest speakers delivered their messages including General Superintendent of Assemblies of God USA Rev Dr Douglas Clay who preaching on the Topic "THE CHURCH AND THE HOLY SPIRIT ", where he talked about the need for forgiveness as christians. After his sermon he led the congregation into prayers and evidence of speaking in other tongues was seen everywhere as the power of God filled the whole Auditorium.

The Arrival of Rev Dr Uma Ukpai to the Grand Finale of the 2019 PENIEL at the National Convention Center of Assemblies of God Nigeria EVANGEL CAMP Okpoto was the highlight of the day, as he was welcomed by the General Superintendent and the Executive Commitee. Rev Dr Uma Ukpai, who gave the last message of the Peniel 2019 program, spoke on a topic titled "THE END TIME CONCERN". In his message he highlighted the need for christians to work with God saying "The Greatest life of a child of God is a walk with God through out your life for God".

After the last message by Rev Dr Uma Ukpai ,the General Superintendent Assemblies of God Nigeria Rev Chidi Okoroafor called the three officers and EC Members to come and bless the congregation. They released prophetic declarations to the people after that the he prayed for the congregation and online viewers for God's blessings upon them.
The program was round up with a music night of praise and worship, where different zones came with their choir to sing to the Glory of God.
