Rev Dr Chidi Okoroafor receives Leadership Award

The General Superintendent, Assemblies of God Nigeria Rev Dr Chidi Okoroafor on the 15th of august received a Leadership Award as ICON OF SOCIETAL TRANSFORMATION IN AFRICA by Africa Students Union Parliament (ASUP) , Pan-African distinguished leadership Honor. This is because of his contributions in promoting African moral values, strong family life, corporation, international harmony, renew of the Pan-Africanism Ideology, a responsible public media and national and Religious barriers.

During the visit The Deputy Speaker wth his team handed over the award to the GS Rev Dr Chidi Okoroafor. Comrade Ozim Temple on behalf of Deputy Speaker thanked The GS and the other Executive members Rev Pst. Ejikeme Ejim (AGS) Rev Vincent Alaje (GT) and Rev Dr Godwin Amaowoh (GSec) for accepting to partner with them in eradicating drugs Abuse and other vices among the youth and Teenagers in Africa.

The AGS told them that in all they do, they should give their lives to Christ, if they have not because for them to achieve more they need Christ in their lives. The GT and GSec also thanked them and gave them advice.

He stated that every effort that is put to rescue youths and teenagers without the word of God will amount to nothing. But with the word God it is easier because the Word of God can break a Hardened sinner. The GS later prayed for them.

List of Delegates are:
1. Hon. Hakeem Kouwsi (Universite De Kokodi, Abijan Ivory Cost) Deputy Speaker.
2. Hon. Adjeipablo Frimpong (University of Legon, Ghana) Chairman, House Committee on Award & Privilages.
3. Comrade Francis Owen (Uniiversity of Abuja).
4. Comrade Ozim Temple (Federal University of Technology, Minna) Deputy Clerk.
5. Comrade Angela Onoha (Nnamdi Azikwe University Awka) Chairman House Committee Gender.
