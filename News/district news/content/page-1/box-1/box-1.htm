District Women's Rally & Evangelism

The exceptional women of the Ikwerre West district of Assemblies of God Nigeria hosted their first district women's rally for the year 2020, on the 19th of March. It was truly a wonderful event as women from different parts of the State came together in their bright colours to celebrate life, good health and all the beautiful things the Lord has done for them.

The program started off with a morning evangelism session, where the women marched out in mass to create and share the gospel. After the evangelism session they all returned back to the church venue which was assemblies of God rumuokwuta (sectional headquarters).

Activities continued in the church where there were several sections of praise, worship and special presentations. The district women's choir also ministered in songs as they blessed the entire congregation with their presentation.

One of the highlights of the events for the day was the special welcome presentation offered by the women to honour their guest for the day Rev. John Vitalis, the district supretenent of ikwere west. The welcome presentation involve a parade show where they marched and sang welcome songs in honour of the guest.

Rounding up the program was a sermon session which was taken by the wife of the district superintendent Rev. Mrs Judith John, where she preached on the topic 'Pentecostal inheritance'. In her message, she urged and stressed the need for the Assemblies of God church at large to return to their past glory and revive the power of God upon the church as it was before. After the message, the district superintendent was called upon to conclude the sermon with prayers. During this prayer session the power of God was evident as there were several prophecies and many were filled with the power of the holy ghost.

In the end it was a successful event and all glory be to God.
